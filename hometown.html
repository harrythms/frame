Kottayam - The Land of Letters, Latex, and Lakes

About Kottayam

Kottayam is known for its literacy, rubber plantations, and stunning backwaters. It serves as a gateway to Kumarakom, a famous backwater destination, and has a rich cultural and historical significance.

Tourist Attractions

  • Kumarakom Backwaters
  • Vembanad Lake
  • Illikkal Kallu
  • Thirunakkara Mahadeva Temple
  • Bay Island Driftwood Museum

Culture and Heritage

Kottayam is renowned for its contributions to Malayalam literature, rubber cultivation, and ancient churches, making it a unique blend of history and nature.

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Kottayam is from October to March when the weather is pleasant and ideal for sightseeing.

How to Reach

By Air: The nearest airport is Cochin International Airport.

By Train: Kottayam Railway Station connects the city to major destinations.

By Road: Kottayam has a well-developed road network linking it to major cities in Kerala.
